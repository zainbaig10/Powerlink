Who We Are

Powerlink Trading Establishment, founded in 2008, is a trusted name in Saudi Arabia's industrial sector. We provide comprehensive solutions in construction, electrical & instrumentation, fire & safety systems, and logistics & equipment rental.

With a client-focused approach and proven industry experience, Powerlink has become a reliable partner to leading organizations across the oil & gas, infrastructure, and public works sectors.

Our Mission

To drive our clients' long-term success by delivering high-quality products and practical solutions that enhance performance. We aim to add value through expert implementation, seamless change management, and a commitment to reliability and excellence.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in industrial supply and contracting, known for delivering innovative, reliable, and high-quality solutions that support the growth of Saudi Arabia's vital sectors.
